Council of Elders
	
	May  deal with cases of juvenile misbehavior before it gets to the police.
	
	May help with domestic issues among members.
	
	All members must be kind and trustworthy with children.
	
	Council may provide a temporary safe haven while matter is being resolved
	before state or county authorities get involved.
	
	Have a small budget from contributions and very small quarterly dues.
	
	Manager the property responsibly.
	
	Maintain good information flow to residents about what is going on, 
	
	Facilitate the organization of simple social events which would appeal to everyone
	and encourage people getting to know their neighbors.
	
	Promote peace and good feelings within the resident community.
